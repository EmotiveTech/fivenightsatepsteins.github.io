About Five Nights at Epstein's

Step into the most horrifying nights in indie horror history! Five Nights at Epstein's is a survival horror game with a darkly satirical edge, where you play as a victim trapped on an abandoned island of power. You have no weapons, no escape. All you have is a small room, a flickering security system, and one single goal: survive five harrowing nights until rescue arrives.

What makes this game so haunting? It’s the sheer isolation of facing a living past within these walls. Nightfall turns the island into a hunting ground. You must constantly monitor 11 distorted camera feeds, use audio lures to redirect your stalkers, and make split-second decisions. Every mistake has a cost, and once you’re found, it’s over.

More than just a horror game, this is a tactical survival challenge that demands strategy and nerves of steel. Blending classic mechanics from Five Nights at Freddy’s with a unique setting, the game delivers escalating tension by the minute, where every footstep echoing down the hall could be your last.

How To Play Five Nights at Epstein's

The rules seem simple, but the difficulty escalates with each passing night. You are stationary in your hideout, interacting only with the control panel and a network of 11 cameras across the island. Your job is to neutralize distinct threats using very different methods.

Controls

  • Mouse: Move to select cameras on screen and survey each area.
  • Control Panel: Your command center for survival. Use it to restart broken cameras or close/open air vents.
  • Audio Lure: Activate this on the camera interface to produce sound, drawing enemies away from your location.

Night 1 – Facing Epstein

He starts at Camera 11.

  • Tip: Constantly play sounds on cameras near his current position to lure him away.
  • Warning: Repeating audio in the same spot will overload and break the camera. You must use the Control Panel to fix it immediately.

Night 2 – Trump Enters

He attacks through the ventilation system connected to Camera 1 and Camera 2.

  • Reaction: Hear banging in the vents? Immediately close the vent covers on the Control Panel.
  • Critical: After a few seconds, you'll hear banging again—he's gone. Reopen the vents immediately or you will suffocate. Always prioritize Epstein first.

Night 3 – Stephen Hawking

He remains stationary at Camera 6 and is immune to all audio lures.

  • Task: You must electrocute him periodically to keep him in place. If ignored, he will leave Camera 6 and head your way.

Night 4 & 5 – Chaos and Finale

All three enemies are active simultaneously with drastically increased speed and aggression.

  • System failures are constant; power drains at an alarming rate.
  • Golden Rule: A single second of delay in rebooting cameras or toggling vents can end your run.

Extra Challenges & Custom Survival Modes

Surviving the five main nights isn't the end of the road.

Night 6 – The Epstein Encore

All old rules are useless. Epstein becomes utterly unpredictable. He can appear anywhere without warning, with erratic behavior and blinding speed. This is the ultimate test of your sanity.

Custom Night

Here, you control the rules. Face any combination of enemies: Epstein, Trump, Stephen, or the mysterious Golden Hawking—a variant that brings crushing pressure. Every AI difficulty level is adjustable, creating endless survival scenarios. Want to train your reflexes against Trump alone? Or face all three at max aggression? Custom Night lets you do it all.

FAQs

Is Five Nights at Epstein's completely free to play?

Yes! You can experience all five nightmare nights and Custom Night modes without any payment. The game is designed for instant browser access.

Can I play on my mobile device?

Absolutely. The mouse-driven interface is optimized for touchscreens, allowing easy camera selection and panel controls with simple taps.

How do I survive Night 3 with Stephen Hawking?

The biggest mistake is forgetting about him. Build a habit of checking Camera 6 after every encounter with Epstein or Trump. One quick electrocution is enough to "lock" him in place. Don't wait for a warning.

Why do my cameras keep going dark?

You're overusing the audio lure. This is especially critical on Nights 4 and 5. A better strategy is to lure Epstein away with 2-3 sounds, then let him move naturally, instead of spamming the lure.

Any tips for absolute beginners?

Don't try to win Night 1 immediately. Spend your first few runs just learning the 11-camera map and Epstein's movement patterns. Once you anticipate his route, you'll know exactly when to use the lure. And remember: stay calm when you see his silhouette. Panic leads to misclicks, and misclicks get you caught.

Are you ready to face the darkness on this island of the damned? Brace yourself, power up the cameras, and try to hold on until dawn!
